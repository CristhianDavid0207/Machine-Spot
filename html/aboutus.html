History

Machine Spot, born from the ingenious collaboration of four software development students in 2024, emerges as a response to the growing need for flexible access to tools and machinery. During an academic project, these entrepreneurs identified the challenge people face when trying to materialize their projects due to the lack of access to the right tools. Under construction, Machine Spot stands as a catalyst for transformation in the way people approach their projects. The founders are committed not only to providing tools, but to building a connected community where the sharing of knowledge, skills and resources becomes the lifeblood of the platform. As Machine Spot evolves, it is anticipated that their story will become an inspiring testament to how a student idea can flourish and fundamentally change the dynamics of project creation.

Misssion

Machine Spot seeks to be the leading platform in Colombia, revolutionizing the way people access and share tools and machinery for projects, thus driving creative development and efficient collaboration nationwide.

Vission

Our mission is to provide a local and innovative solution that simplifies access to tools and machinery, enabling users to carry out projects in an affordable and sustainable way. We are dedicated to building a collaborative community that drives progress and the realization of ideas across the country.
